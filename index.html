Behind the Curtain

Our Story

Tony & Tiffany Hall conjured Hall's Balls & Exotics in 2019, born from a shared passion for nature and the animals most folks fear. What started small has grown into a family-owned pet shop in Martinez's Marketplace West — and the very first ball python they ever kept is still a beloved member of the family today.

We're breeders first, with our hearts set on healthy, beautiful Ball Pythons and Crested Geckos — and Carpet Pythons and Boas joining the menagerie soon. Tiffany came up mentored by the reptile community, and we pay it forward: honest guidance, a proper setup, and a standing invitation to come back with questions. No hustle. No mystery boxes. Just healthy animals and keepers who genuinely give a damn.
